Issues are unpredictable. Finding a pro last minute in NYC is stressful and expensive. We ensure that when something breaks, you don't have to scramble or worry about the bill.
❓ Q2: What if I don't use it this month? Is it wasted?
It's like insurance. You don't want to use it, but it's there to prevent a massive bill. Plus, you can request routine maintenance anytime.
❓ Q3: Is labor truly free?
Yes. Standard labor for our included services is $0. You only pay for the actual cost of parts.

Never. We charge parts at cost and always confirm the price with you before starting any work.

Major structural renovations or projects requiring specialized licensed contractors (like gut renos) are not covered by the standard labor plan, but we can help coordinate them.
